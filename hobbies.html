My Hobbies

Photography

One of my favorite hobbies is film photography. I really enjoy the whole process, from loading a roll of film, to shooting it, then developing it, and then scanning it. Currently I do not develop my own film but that is something I plan on doing in the near future. I really enjoy finally getting to see my photos once they are uploaded to an online album. There is so much anticipation because I dont get to see my photots right after I took, I have to wait. Its a good feeling seeing a properly exposed photo knowing I metered the light correctlty. I am having so much fun go out and about using different film stocks and learning what each one has to offer. Photography has been an so much fun to learn and will continue this hobby into my old age.
